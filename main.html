What are Unicorns?

Unicorns are defined as privately held startup companies that are valued at over US$1 billion, and are of many industries, including Artificial Intelligence (same colour), Fintech, Health, and many more.

Over all unicorn industries, Fintech is the leading industry with a total of $794.82 billion in valuation.
